A self-contained sports betting analytics dashboard. Pulls live odds from major sportsbooks (DraftKings, FanDuel, BetMGM, Caesars, etc.) and gives you tools to find value, size bets correctly, build parlays, lock in profit on hedges, and settle group-chat arguments with a coin flip.

Tab quick reference:

  • Bet of the day — Auto-picks the highest-EV bet from current odds. Screenshot-ready card.
  • Live odds — Compare prices across all books. Best price highlighted in green.
  • Value finder — Scans for +EV bets vs. consensus fair line.
  • Arbitrage — Two-way arbs where you can lock in profit.
  • Parlay builder — Combine bets, see combined odds, payout, and EV.
  • Kelly — Tells you how much to bet given your edge.
  • Hedge — Lock in profit on a live ticket.
  • Odds converter — American ↔ Decimal ↔ Implied probability.
  • Consensus & vig — Ranks books by juice.
  • Coin flip — Settle group chat arguments fairly.

Two-way arbs: when the best price for side A on one book plus the best price for side B on another adds up to less than 100% implied probability, you can bet both sides and lock in profit no matter who wins.

Worked Example · $100 bankroll

Suppose Oilers vs Flames moneyline:

  • DraftKings has Oilers +130 (43.5% implied)
  • FanDuel has Flames +110 (47.6% implied)

Total implied probability: 91.1% → that's an 8.9% arb.

How to size the bets:

Stake on Oilers (DK) = $100 × (47.6 / 91.1) = $52.25
Stake on Flames (FD) = $100 × (43.5 / 91.1) = $47.75
Total wagered = $100.00

Either way you win:

  • If Oilers win: $52.25 × 2.30 = $120.18 back → +$20.18 profit
  • If Flames win: $47.75 × 2.10 = $100.28 back → +$0.28 profit

Note: real arbs are rarely this clean — most are 0.5–2%, locking in $1–3 on $100 risked. Still beats losing.

Quick reference:
−110 means bet $110 to win $100 (52.4% implied)
+100 = even money, double your money on a win (50% implied)
+200 means bet $100 to win $200 (33.3% implied)
−200 means bet $200 to win $100 (66.7% implied)

"Vig" or "juice" is the book's built-in margin. Sharp books like Pinnacle run 2–4% vig; soft US books often 5–8%. Lower vig = closer to true odds.
